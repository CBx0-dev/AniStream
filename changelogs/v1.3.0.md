# v1.3.0

This release brings a suite of new tools designed to give you more control over how you organize and discover content.
This update focuses on personalization through custom lists and seamless background updates with our new auto-sync
feature, ensuring your catalog is always ready for your next session.

## New features

- You can now create, view, and manage your own custom series lists.
- Added a setting to automatically synchronize the catalog on startup.

## Fixed

- Fixed an issue where the dropdown menu in the episode list was not displaying correctly.
- Series that are completely watched are now filtered out from the "Where you left of" list.
- Performing security audits of project dependencies and updating them
